About Me

Hello! My name is Hema Teja. I was born on June 18, 2007, in Hyderabad. Coming from a middle-class family, I consider myself an introvert who enjoys trying new things, listening to songs, and exploring new opportunities. Despite experiencing two breakups, I am currently single.

On May 29, 2019, I founded AirMe Organisation (AO), driven by my passion for startups and entrepreneurship. I am fortunate to work with two incredible friends and teammates: Sriya (also known as Anu), the co-founder, programmer, and team manager, and Deepak, our talented video editor. In my professional circles, I am known as Ādhipa Surya.

AirMe Organisation collaborates with new startups and entrepreneurs, offering tools and services such as Shama Services, Dotage Language, Stella51483 Software, and many more. We proudly own over 14 brands and hold stakes in several others. Partnering with us provides exclusive access to our products at free or reduced rates, and we offer unwavering support during challenging times. We believe in the power of collaboration to drive success.

About AirMe Organization

Unleashing Creativity for Developers and Creators!

Welcome to AirMe Organization, your go-to hub for all things creative and techy! Founded on May 14, 2020, and officially launched on May 29, we're not your typical group – we're a bunch of tech enthusiasts who are here to empower developers and creators like you.

How We Roll

At AirMe Organization, we're not just a team; we're a crew of developers and creators making cool things happen. We're students from regular families, just like you, using the internet and smart tech to simplify work and learn new skills.
